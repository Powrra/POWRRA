YOUR WELL IS AT RISK

If you own property with a well in Whatcom County's Nooksack Basin (WRIA 1), your well could be:

  • Rank your existing well as "junior" to tribal government claims
  • Require metering, reporting, and possible shut-downs during dry periods
  • Reduce property value due to water uncertainty
  • Force you into settlement without testing legal claims in court

WHY WE CHALLENGE THIS

POWWRA's Position: The state's claim is legally and historically false.

  • Individual Rights: 1855 Treaty allotted lands to individuals, not tribal governments
  • State Jurisdiction: Federal laws (Burke Act, PL 280) placed lands under state jurisdiction
  • No Proof: Ecology hasn't proven which lands are validly in federal trust

WHAT YOU MUST DO NOW

1. DON'T File Alone

Filing with the state without legal counsel could make you lose your rights. Join POWWRA's coordinated legal challenge instead.

2. Join POWWRA Now

Connect with property owners fighting back through coordinated lawsuit. Strength in numbers.

3. Attend Meetings

Learn your options before May 1, 2026 deadline. Get connected with water rights attorneys.

4. Spread the Word

Tell neighbors. Print this flyer. Many property owners don't know this is happening.
