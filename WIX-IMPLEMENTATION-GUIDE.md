## PHASE 1: CREATE WIX ACCOUNT & CHOOSE TEMPLATE (30 minutes)

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. **Go to:** Wix.com
2. **Click:** "Get Started"
3. **Create account** (email + password)

### Step 2: Choose How to Build

Wix will ask: "How do you want to create your website?"

**Choose:** "Pick a template" (NOT Wix ADI - you want manual control)

### Step 3: Select Template Category

**Choose:** "Community & Education" or "Events"

**Look for templates with these features:**
- Clean, professional layout
- Prominent call-to-action buttons
- Homepage hero section
- Easy navigation
- Mobile-responsive

**Recommended Templates:**
- **"Advocacy Group"** - perfect for your cause
- **"Community Organization"**
- **"Fundraising Campaign"**
- **"Legal Services"** - professional look

**Preview several, then click "Edit" on your favorite**

### Step 4: Enter Wix Editor

You're now in the **Wix Editor** - this is where you'll build everything.

**Take 5 minutes to explore:**
- Left panel: Pages, Add Elements, Media, etc.
- Top bar: Save, Preview, Publish
- Canvas: Your actual website (drag and drop!)
- Right panel: Settings for selected element
