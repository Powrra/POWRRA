YOUR WELL IS AT RISK

If you own property with a well in Whatcom County's Nooksack Basin (WRIA 1), a lawsuit has been filed that could:

  • Rank your well as "junior" to tribal claims
  • Require metering and possible shut-downs
  • Reduce property value
  • Force settlement without testing claims in court

Why We Challenge This

  • Individual Rights: 1855 Treaty allotted lands to individuals, not tribal governments
  • State Jurisdiction: Federal laws (Burke Act, PL 280) placed lands under state jurisdiction
  • No Proof: Ecology hasn't proven which lands are validly in federal trust

What You Must Do

1. File Your Claim

File by May 1, 2026 or risk losing rights. Consult an attorney.

2. Challenge Jurisdiction

Reserve your right to challenge tribal trust claims.

3. Join the Fight

Connect with POWWRA. Contribute to legal defense fund.

4. Spread the Word

Tell neighbors. Attend meetings. Print this flyer.
