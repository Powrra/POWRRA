## WHY WE CHALLENGE THIS

**POWWRA's Position: The state's claim is legally and historically false.**

- **Individual Rights:** 1855 Treaty allotted lands to individual Indians, not corporate tribal governments.
- **State Jurisdiction:** Federal laws (Burke Act, PL 280) placed allotted lands under state/county jurisdiction.
- **Supreme Court Precedent:** Cases (Goudy v. Meath, Winters v. US) don't support blanket tribal trust claims.
- **No Proof:** Ecology has not proven which specific lands are validly held in federal trust status today.
